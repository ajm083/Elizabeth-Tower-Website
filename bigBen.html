What about the bells?

Home

It is a common misconception that the tower is called Big Ben, but this is actually the name of the main bell.

There is the main bell which is called Big Ben and there are quarter bells numbered one through four. Each bell has its own musical note to create the iconic chimes.

Below is the corresponding notes to the chimes of the bells:

Bell Note
Big Ben E
First Quarter Bell G
Second Quarter Bell F#
Third Quarter Bell E
Fourth Quarter Bell B
